How To Play

🖱️Click any colored cell to explode it along with all connected cells of the same color. You need at least 2 connected cells to trigger a chain.
🎯Clear the color targets shown at the top before you run out of moves to advance to the next level.
⬇️Gravity kicks in after each explosion — remaining cells fall downward, creating new connections you can chain together!
💥Bigger chains = more points. Score = (cells)² × 10 × level multiplier. Clearing the full board gives a +500 bonus!
📈Each level introduces more colors and bigger required groups, making it progressively harder.
